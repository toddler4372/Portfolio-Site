About Me

Stuff about me

My name is Todd Williams and I am a Front End Developer and Designer living in Redlands, CA. My passions are my family, guitar, tennis, gaming, technology, and web development. I take great pride in my work, am extremely focused, and always meet my deadlines. I have over 10 years of experience as a digital artist, having transitioned from the video game industry to web development in 2009. My most recent work reflects my transition to Responsive Web Design, and I am currently really liking Google's new Material Design spec.
